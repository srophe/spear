Events

Severus wrote a letter to Philoxenus on the interpretation of 1 Corinthians 3.10. See factoid page

Sometime between 513 and 518 , Severus sent a letter to Philoxenus of Mabbug detailing his request to Philoxenus , of Doliche that Romulus , be granted permission to conduct prayers in Antioch . See factoid page

Non-Chalcedonians in Isauria opposed Philoxenus because he held that the Henotikon was sufficient for communion between Christians. See factoid page

In 518/19 A. Gr. 830 Justin deposed Severus and Philoxenos for refusing to accept the Council of Chalcedon . See factoid page
