Event Factoid: Paul, patriarch of Antioch organized the ministry to the poor in Antioch and then went to other great cities, both eastern and western, encouraging, organizing, and instituting this kind of ministry through Christian people who concerned themselves about their lives' salvation, and earnestly endeavoured to gain the inheritance of eternal life.
