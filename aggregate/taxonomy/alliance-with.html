SPEAR Factoids about Alliance with

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/taxonomy/alliance-with

relation: Severus of Antioch, Eleusinius and Proclus formed an alliance. See factoid page

event: Anthimus sent letters to Severus and Theodosius who sent synodical letters to him as well. They agreed to a union in opposition to the Council of Chalcedon. See factoid page

relation: Severus of Antioch, Antoninus, Constantine, Cassian, Timothy IV and Paul had an alliance. See factoid page
