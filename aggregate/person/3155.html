SPEAR Factoids about Tribunus — ܛܪܝܒܘܢܐ

Tribunus was a devout layman and ascetic disciple whose life was recorded in John of Ephesus' Lives of the Eastern Saints [http://syriaca.org/work/9501] . 1

Events

Tribunus resists marriage fearing that such a worldly attachment would lead to sin and the begetting of children who would sin and die. See factoid page
