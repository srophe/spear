SPEAR Factoids about Constantine the Great — ܩܘܣܛܢܛܝܢܘܣ

Constantine the Great the Great was the Roman Emperor from 306 to 307 AD and is honored in Christian memory on account of the Edict of Milan in 313 AD which granted religious tolerance in the Roman Empire.
