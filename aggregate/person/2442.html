Relationships

Stephen was a bishop with authority over Anonymi 3746 See factoid page

Events

Sometime in the early 520s Anonymi 3108 and Anonymi 3105 in Isauria suffered persecution and expulsion at the hands of Stephen. Anonymi 3108 went into exile. See factoid page

Sometime in the early 520s Stephen sent Anonymi 3746 to bring back Anonymi 3108 who had fled. See factoid page

Stephen was ordained bishop of Seleucia in Isauria. See factoid page

Stephen sent a letter to Severus assuring him of his orthodox beliefs. See factoid page
