A presbyter from Minidus who doubted whether those who lapsed into heresy, but were forgiven, ought to return to their former grade in the ministry3

Personal Information

Occupation(s):

Gennadius was a presbyter in Minidus. See factoid page

Events

Nicias wrote a letter to Severus informing him that Gennadius doubted that heretical clergy who have repented could be received. See factoid page
