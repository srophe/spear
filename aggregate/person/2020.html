SPEAR Factoids about Polycarp of Smyrna — ܦܘܠܝܩܪܦܘܣ

Polycarp of Smyrna was an early bishop of Smyrna who was martyred as an old man in the second century. His story became a model for passion narratives of Christian martyrs. 1
