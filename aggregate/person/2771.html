Personal Information

Place of residence:

Thomas of Dara resided in Marde. See factoid page

Sanctity:

Thomas of Dara was a confessor. See factoid page

Occupation(s):

Thomas of Dara was a bishop. See factoid page

Events

Severus wrote a letter to John of Tella, Philoxenus of Doliche, and Thomas of Dara on the subject of receiving into communion those baptized in another confession. See factoid page
