Relationships

Harfat and Anonymous 3764 were kinsmen. See factoid page

Anonymi 3765 were enslaved by Anonymous 3764 and Harfat. See factoid page

Events

Harfat and his kinsman Anonymi 3764 inherited their family's considerable wealth. See factoid page

Harfat was distressed by the exploitation of Anonymi 3765 and Anonymi 3766 by his kinsman Anonymi 3764. He does not approve of the great wealth of the estate they inherited. See factoid page

Anonymi 3764, though a layman, used his wealth to become bishop of Arsamosata. See factoid page

Harfat wanted to divide the family wealth and property in order to give it to the poor. His kinsman Anonymi 3764 refused. Harfat took 5000 denarii cash and distributed it to the poor. Then he withdrew to a monastery. See factoid page
