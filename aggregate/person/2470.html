Events

Paul of Ephesus rejected the Chalcedonian confession and joined an encyclical declaring miaphysite Christology. See factoid page

Timothy II, Paul of Ephesus, Peter of Antioch, and Anastasius of Jerusalem co-authored an encyclical letter condemning heresy. See factoid page

Paul of Ephesus and Timothy of Alexandria communed together after the miaphysite confession of Paul. See factoid page
