Sanctity:

Events

Simeon also battled the Magian heresy and converted three distinguished Magians to Christianity. See factoid page

The King was informed about the three distinguished Magians who converted to Christianity and ordered them to renounce Christianity on pain of death. See factoid page

Anonymi 3618 refused to renounce Christianity and were put to the sword. They became martyrs. See factoid page
