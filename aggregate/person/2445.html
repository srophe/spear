Personal Information

Name variant(s):

Eutherius of Tyana See factoid page

Sex:

Eutherius was male. See factoid page

Relationships

Severus of Antioch had enmity for Dorotheus, Eutherius, Himerius and Helladius. See factoid page

Paul, Dorotheus, Eutherius, Himerius and Helladius formed an alliance. See factoid page

Events

After the Council of Ephesus, Paul petitioned Cyril of Alexandria to grant forgiveness to Dorotheus, Eutherius, Himerius, and Helladius. For the sake of church unity, Cyril of Alexandria agreed on the condition that they condemn Nestorius. See factoid page
