SPEAR Factoids about Jacob the Cut-Up — ܝܥܩܘܒ ܡܦܣܩܐ

Jacob "the cut-up" was a noble Persian from the Royal City of Beth Lapaṭ. He became a Christian, but he later abjured his Christian identity. His family reproached him, so he confessed his faith before Bahram V and was martyred.
