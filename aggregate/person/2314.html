SPEAR Factoids about Anonymi 2314 — The Bishops assembled at the Second Council of Ephesus in 429

An anonymous person or group of persons mentioned in the The Chronicle of Edessa (circa mid 6th century).

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/person/2314

Personal Information

Name variant(s):

The bishops assembled at the Second Council of Ephesus in A. D. 429 See factoid page

Relationships

Anonymi 2314 had enmity for Flavian, Domnus of Antioch, Irenaeus of Tyre, Hiba of Edessa, Eusebius of Dorylaeum, Daniel of Harran, Silwana and Theodoret of Cyrrhus. See factoid page

Events

The bishops assembled at the Second Council of Ephesus anathematized Flavian of Constantinople , Domnus of Antioch , Irenaeus of Tyre , Hiba of Edessa , Eusebius of Dorylaeum , Daniel of Ḥarran , Sophronius of Tella , and Theodoret of Cyrrhus . See factoid page
