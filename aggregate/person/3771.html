SPEAR Factoids about Anonymi 3771

“The holy community of non-Chalcedonians that the Empress Theodora gathered together in Constantinople” 1

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/person/3771

Personal Information

Place of residence:

Anonymi 3771 resided in the palace of Hormisdas. See factoid page

Sanctity:

Anonymi 3771 was a group of saints. See factoid page

Relationships

John of Ephesus commemmorated Anonymi 3771 See factoid page

Events

The number of visitors to the holy men in the palace of Hormisdas resulted in the floor collapsing. Thanks to the prayers of the saints and the mercy of God, no one was injured. See factoid page

Theodora commissioned the refurbishing of the palace of Hormisdas so that Anonymi 3771 could live there as in a monastery, performing acts of prayer and asceticism. See factoid page

Even those who disagreed with the Christology of Anonymi 3771 marveled at their asceticism and came to the palace of Hormisdas to venerate them. Some even renounced the Council of Chalcedon. See factoid page
