SPEAR Factoids about Adelphius — ܐܕܠܦܝܘܣ

The source of an antionomian heresy with followers in Armenia, Cappadocia, Pontus, and Galatia2

Personal Information

Name variant(s):

Adelphius See factoid page

Sex:

Adelphius was male. See factoid page

Relationships

Lampetius was a follower of Adelphius. See factoid page

Anonymi 3051 brought legal charges or a petition against Adelphius. See factoid page

Anonymi 3052 had enmity for Adelphius. See factoid page

Events

Procopius had attempted to profit off his ordinations, and had ordained Lampetius, a known follower of the heresy of Adelphius which had been condemned by the synod at Commanus in Armenia. See factoid page

The heresy of Adelphius had been condemned by Soteric of Caesarea and the bishops of Cappadocia, the bishops of Pontus, and the bishops of Galatia. See factoid page
