SPEAR Factoids about James of the Monastery of the Edessenes

In hagiography: James of the Monastery of the Edessenes is a wonderworking monk originally from Monastery of the Edessenes near Amida who was commemorated as one of "two monks" (the other unnamed) by John of Ephesus. He is mentioned as a contemporary who met with John of Tella.2
