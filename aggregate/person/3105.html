SPEAR Factoids about Anonymi 3105 — The non-Chalcedonian monks, clergy, and laity of Isauria who suffered persecution in the early 520s

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/person/3105

Events

Sometime in the early 520s Anonymi 3108 and Anonymi 3105 in Isauria suffered persecution and expulsion at the hands of Stephen. Anonymi 3108 went into exile. See factoid page
