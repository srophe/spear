SPEAR Factoids about Anastasios I

“Anastasius ruled the Roman Empire from 491 to 518. The wealth of the imperial treasury increased considerably during his reign. He was the last emperor who did not insist on the orthodoxy of the council of Chalcedon and, in his last years, allowed the accession of anti-Chalcedonian bishops to major episcopal sees, such as Severus in Antioch.” 8
