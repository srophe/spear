“An Ethiopian monk from the monastery of Romanus” 1

Personal Information

Ethnic Label:

Anonymous 3784 was Ethiopian. See factoid page

Occupation(s):

Anonymous 3784 was a monk at the Monastery of Romanus. See factoid page

Relationships

Eupraxius was an archimandrite with authority over Anonymous 3784. See factoid page

Events

After years of living as a monk and regularly taking communion, Anonymous 3784 witnessed a baptism for the first time and admitted that he had never been baptized. After conducting a formal investigation in order to ensure that they avoid re-baptism, Anonymous 3784 was baptized. See factoid page
