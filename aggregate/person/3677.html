Personal Information

Name variant(s):

Anonymi 3677 of the Convent of John Urtaya who accompanied Abba into exile. See factoid page

Sex:

Anonymi 3677 were male. See factoid page

Place of residence:

Anonymi 3677 who followed Abba into exile resided in the Convent of the Poplars. See factoid page

Anonymi 3677 who went into exile with Abba resided in the Convent of Nut. See factoid page

Anonymi 3677 who followed Abba into exile resided in the Monastery of the Huts. See factoid page

Occupation(s):

Anonymi 3677 were monks. See factoid page

Relationships

Abba was a monastic authority over Anonymi 3677. See factoid page

John was a monastic authority over Samuel, Jonathan and Anonymi 3677. See factoid page

Events

Abba and Anonymi 3677 went into exile and took up residence in the Convent of the Poplars. See factoid page

Abba and Anonymi 3677 were expelled from the Convent of the Poplars and took up residence in the Convent of Nut in the village of 'Bdhyr. See factoid page
