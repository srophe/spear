Events

Between A.D. 521 and 526 the community was forced to leave their monastery by the defenders of the corrupt synod of Chalcedon. The community was so large, that the monastery of Mar Mama in the village of Hzyn in Ṭyšf could not hold them. See factoid page

A group exiled of monks welcome everyone in the village of Hzyn. See factoid page

Abbi became a monk in a community in the village of Ḥzyn. See factoid page

Abbi lived nine years in the monastic community at the village of Ḥzyn. See factoid page

Residence:

Abbi resided in the village of Ḥzyn in the district of Ṭyšf. See factoid page
