Events

At the summons of Justinian, Severus traveled to Constantinople where he taught and advocated for the non-Chalcedonian cause. After two years he decided it was futile are returned to his exile outside Alexandria. See factoid page

After the Council of Chalcedon , John was expelled from Gaza, and traveled into Alexandria, finally settling in the Ennaton. See factoid page

When Stephen united with bishops in the regions of Palestine and Alexandria he opposed those who insisted that Chalcedonians who repented of their theology needed to be re-baptized or re-annointed. See factoid page
