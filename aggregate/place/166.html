SPEAR Factoids about Roman Empire

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/place/166

Events

In February A. D. 364 Shebāṭ A. Gr. 675 Valentinian became King of Rome . See factoid page

In March 364 February A. D. 364 Shebāṭ A. Gr. 675 Valens became King of Rome . See factoid page

In July, 395 Tammuz A. Gr. 706 the Huns entered Roman territory . See factoid page

In 403/4 A. Gr. 715 ʿAbsmayyā composed madrāshe on the Huns entering Roman territory . See factoid page

Sometime between 519 and 525 there was a barbarian incursion into Roman territory. See factoid page

After 9 July 518 9 Tammuz A. Gr. 829 Justin became King of Rome . See factoid page

In 44 B.C. 46/5 B.C. A. Gr. 266 Augustus became King of Rome . See factoid page

On 10 August A. D. 527 10 Āb A. Gr. 838 Justinian became the sole King of Rome . See factoid page

On 18 December A. D. 531 18 Kānun I A. Gr. 843 the Huns entered Roman territory and plundered as far as the area around Aleppo and Antioch . See factoid page

In September A. D. 532 Elul A. Gr. 843 Rufinus made peace between Rome , and Persia . See factoid page

There was peace between Rome and Persia from September 532 to May 540 Elul A. Gr. 843 to Iyār A. Gr. 851 See factoid page

In May 540 Iyār A. Gr. 851 Chosroes broke the peace and invaded Roman territory . He laid waste Shura , Aleppo , and Antioch . The following factoids deal with closely related events: 8559-538, 8559-542 See factoid page

Simeon travelled through Persia and into Rome in order to convert more people to Christianity. See factoid page
