SPEAR Factoids about East — ܡܕܢܚܐ

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/place/2604

Events

When working to reunite Egypt and the East after the Council of Ephesus Paul brokered an agreement that conversion from Nestorianism required only an orthodox profession of faith without the need for re-baptism or re-anointing. See factoid page

John and John sent a letter to Severus of Antioch discussing the persecution of non-Chalcedonians in the East, and on the ordination of those who originally received ordination from heretics but wish to join the non-Chalcedonians. See factoid page

Non-Chalcedonians in the East accept converts from Chalcedonianism without re-baptism, or re-anointing, re-ordination. See factoid page

Severus held a council in Antioch with bishops from the East. They condemned the Council of Chalcedon and Leo's Tome. See factoid page

Personal Names:

Those who created divisions between the non-Chalcedonian churches of Egypt and the East during the late 520s See factoid page
