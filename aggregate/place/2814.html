SPEAR Factoids about Mar Mama — ܡܪܝ ܡܡܐ

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/place/2814

Events

Kashish travelled to Constantinople with John of Ephesus, before returning to Chios, where he spent the final years of his life. He was buried in the monastery of Mar Mama. See factoid page

Thomas died in the royal city and was buried in the monastery of Mar Mama. Maria died at the same time in Armenia. See factoid page
