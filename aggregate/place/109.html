SPEAR Factoids about Kallinikos — ܩܐܠܘܢܝܩܝ

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/place/109

Events

After leaving Thella, John returned to his former convent in the city of Callinicius. See factoid page

Elijah, Theodore, and their sisters decided to expand their ministry to the maltreated and persecuted believers in Syria, Arabia, Callinicus, selling everything that they owned. See factoid page

In 465/6 A. Gr. 777 Leo built Kallinikos in Osrhoene and called it Leontopolis . See factoid page
