SPEAR Factoids about Cell of Simeon the Solitary

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/place/2618

Events

Simeon planned and built his cell two miles from Amida See factoid page

Two young men enter the cell of Simeon and are unable to leave or put down the food they have taken until Simeon releases them by prayer. See factoid page

Simeon cooks and lays out food, wine, an inscribed tablet in his cell to welcome strangers. See factoid page

Residence:

Simeon resided in a cell on a mountain two miles from Amida . See factoid page
