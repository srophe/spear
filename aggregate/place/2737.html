Events

John continued to ask the old man for his teaching. In frustruation, the old man left Amida. See factoid page

In 529 840 , John ordained seventy men from the convents of Amida. See factoid page

John was captured and brought to Antioch, where he was tortured and killed by Ephraim of Amida . See factoid page

Members of the church of Amida defended the Jews and prefered accusation against Sergius and Anonymi 3698. See factoid page

After five years in Mar Mara, the community returned to Amida, but because of persecution, relocated to the border territory of the East, near the hot springs of Abarne, settling at the convent of the Poplars. See factoid page
