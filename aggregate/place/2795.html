SPEAR Factoids about Monastery of the Huts — ܕܝܪܐ ܕܟܘܪ̈ܚܐ

Syriaca.org URI: http://syriaca.org/place/2795

Events

Mar Samuel gained distinction in the conflict of persecution. Mar Samuel went into exile in the district of Claudias where he took up residence in the Convent of the Huts. He related the story of the establishment of the Convent of John Urtaya to John of Ephesus. This occurred 536/7 A. Gr. 848 See factoid page

After three years of residing in the Convent of Nut, Abba and his monks went to live in the Monastery of the Huts. See factoid page

Abba returned to the Convent of the Huts and died. See factoid page

Residence:

Mar Samuel resided in the Convent of the Huts in the district of Claudias . See factoid page

Abba resided in the Monastery of the Huts. See factoid page

Anonymi 3677 who followed Abba into exile resided in the Monastery of the Huts. See factoid page
