event: Sergius and Anonymi 3698 put out the fire and built a martyr's chapel on the site of the synagogue. Anonymi 3686 burned down the huts in response and Sergius rebuilt them. See factoid page

event: Sergius and Anonymi 3698 tore down the synagogue that Anonymi 3686 rebuilt two more times before Sergius secluded himself in an enclosure. See factoid page

event: Shortly after Sergius established the monastery of Qlwfyte, his adversaries came to pull it down. Sergius told his enemies that he would rebuild it each time it was destroyed, so they finally left him alone. Sergius rebuilt the monastery of Qlwfyte and invited Simeon to stay with him. Sergius died after Simeon's arrival. See factoid page
